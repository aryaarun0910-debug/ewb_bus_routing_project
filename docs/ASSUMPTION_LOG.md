# Assumption Log — UK2026-82 Predictive Bus Routing

> Rubric target: Criterion 2a, score 5 — *"identifying, verifying and logging assumptions in the team's decision-making."*
> This log names every load-bearing assumption in the pipeline, where it enters the code, its verification status, what happens if it is wrong, and the trigger that would force a revisit.
> Status legend: **VERIFIED** (tested against independent evidence) · **BOUNDED** (untestable directly, but sensitivity-tested and disclosed) · **OPEN** (named, monitored, not yet testable with available data).

## How this log is used
Every entry follows the same loop the rubric asks for: **identify** (named here), **verify** (the Evidence column — six robustness checks, source citations, or explicit bounds), **log** (this file, version-controlled). Open assumptions are not weaknesses hidden in footnotes; they are the project's funded next steps (APC request, pilot survey, CQR intervals).
